Financial Analyst
Sample Finance Company

Responsibilities
- Analyze financial data and develop forecasts to support business decisions
- Prepare financial reports, dashboards, and performance metrics
- Build financial models for budgeting and investment analysis
- Evaluate market trends and economic conditions
- Provide strategic recommendations to management

Required
- Strong financial modeling and Excel skills
- Knowledge of financial statements and valuation methods
- Analytical thinking and attention to detail
- Communication skills for presenting insights to stakeholders

Education
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or Economics
