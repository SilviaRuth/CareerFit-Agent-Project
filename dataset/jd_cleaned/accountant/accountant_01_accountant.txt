Accountant
Sample Accountant Company

Responsibilities
- Prepare and analyze financial statements, including balance sheets and income reports
- Maintain general ledger and reconcile accounts
- Ensure compliance with accounting standards (e.g., GAAP)
- Prepare tax documents and support audits
- Monitor financial performance and identify improvement areas

Required
- Knowledge of accounting principles and regulations (GAAP)
- Proficiency in accounting tools (e.g., QuickBooks, Excel)
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Attention to detail and organizational ability

Education
- Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance
