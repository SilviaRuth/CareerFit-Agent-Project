Fitness Trainer
Sample Fitness Company

Responsibilities
- Design personalized fitness programs based on client goals
- Instruct clients on proper exercise techniques
- Monitor progress and adjust training plans
- Provide motivation and ensure safe workout practices
- Educate clients on health, nutrition, and wellness

Required
- Certification in fitness training (e.g., NASM, ACE, or equivalent)
- Knowledge of anatomy, physiology, and exercise science
- Strong interpersonal and motivational skills
- Ability to demonstrate exercises and techniques
- Passion for health and fitness
