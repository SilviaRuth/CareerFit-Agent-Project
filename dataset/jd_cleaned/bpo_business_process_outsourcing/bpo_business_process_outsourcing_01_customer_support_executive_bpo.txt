Customer Support Executive (BPO)
Sample Bpo Business Process Outsourcing Company

Responsibilities
- Handle inbound/outbound customer calls and resolve inquiries
- Provide product/service information and troubleshoot issues
- Maintain customer records in CRM systems
- Escalate complex issues to higher-level support
- Meet service level agreements (SLAs) and performance metrics

Required
- Strong verbal and written communication skills
- Ability to handle high-volume customer interactions
- Basic computer literacy and CRM tools
- Problem-solving and multitasking ability

Education
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience
