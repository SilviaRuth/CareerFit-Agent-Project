Flight Attendant
Sample Aviation Company

Responsibilities
- Ensure passenger safety and compliance with aviation regulations
- Conduct pre-flight safety checks and demonstrate emergency procedures
- Provide in-flight services (food, beverages, assistance)
- Respond to emergencies and provide first aid if needed
- Maintain cabin order and handle passenger concerns

Required
- Certification from aviation authority (e.g., FAA or equivalent)
- Strong customer service and communication skills
- Physical fitness and ability to handle emergency situations
- Ability to work irregular schedules including nights and weekends

Education
- High school diploma or equivalent (degree )
