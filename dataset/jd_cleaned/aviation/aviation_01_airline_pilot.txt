Airline Pilot
Sample Aviation Company

Responsibilities
- Operate and navigate aircraft according to flight plans and aviation procedures
- Communicate with air traffic control to ensure safe takeoff, flight, and landing
- Monitor weather conditions and adjust routes as necessary
- Perform pre-flight and post-flight safety checks
- Ensure passenger and crew safety throughout the flight

Required
- Valid Airline Transport Pilot (ATP) license or equivalent
- Completion of certified flight training program
- Strong decision-making and situational awareness
- Ability to work under pressure in safety-critical environments
- Excellent communication and teamwork skills
