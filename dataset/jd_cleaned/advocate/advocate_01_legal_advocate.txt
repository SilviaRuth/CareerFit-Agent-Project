Legal Advocate
Sample Advocate Company

Responsibilities
- Represent clients in court proceedings and legal negotiations
- Provide legal advice and explain rights, obligations, and case strategies
- Prepare legal documents such as contracts, briefs, and case filings
- Conduct legal research and analyze case law
- Negotiate settlements and liaise with stakeholders (courts, clients, authorities)

Required
- Licensed to practice law / Bar admission
- Strong knowledge of legal procedures and regulations
- Excellent analytical, negotiation, and communication skills
- High ethical standards and attention to detail

Education
- Bachelor's degree in Law (LLB or equivalent)
